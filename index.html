About

My name is Nathan and I am a web developer! After graduating with a B.A. in Psychology from UC Irvine, I decided to further my knowledge and enter into the tech field through web development. Now I'm making both front-end and back-end applications in order to practice my skills and develop new ones. I hope you get a chance to check out my completed projects, but I'm also continuing to grow and learn to make even better applications in the future.
